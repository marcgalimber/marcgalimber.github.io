Grand Teton National Park (Wyoming)

Grand Teton National Park is located in the Jackson Hole valley in northwestern Wyoming, just 10 miles (16 km) south of Yellowstone's southern entrance. In this park, you can observe an incredible variety of wildlife and admire the stunning Teton Range mountains. The park offers an extensive network of trails suitable for all hiking levels, and in winter it becomes a popular destination for cross-country skiing. Nearby, you’ll also find resorts for downhill skiing, such as the Jackson Hole Mountain Resort.
Culinary tip: Several locations in the park offer both food and souvenirs along with fantastic views of the Tetons – for example the Signal Mountain Lodge.
