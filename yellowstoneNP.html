Grand Teton National Park (Wyoming)

Yellowstone National Park is the oldest national park in the United States (established on March 1, 1872) and is located in three states —Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho. The park offers an extensive network of trails suitable for all hiking levels, and in winter it becomes a popular destination for cross-country skiing. Nearby, you’ll also find resorts for downhill skiing, such as the Jackson Hole Mountain Resort. Culinary suggestion: Several locations in the park offer both food and souvenirs along with fantastic views of the Tetons – for example the Signal Mountain Lodge.
